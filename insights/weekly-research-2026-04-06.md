## 🆕 Happy Found Farm — Competitor Deep Dive

**Website:** [happyfoundfarm.com](https://www.happyfoundfarm.com)
**Facebook:** [facebook.com/happyfoundfarm](https://www.facebook.com/happyfoundfarm/)
**Instagram:** @happyfoundfarm
**Email:** happyfoundfarm@gmail.com
**Location:** Appears to be in or near the CDA/North Idaho area (actively posting in CDA community Facebook groups)

### What They Offer
- **Birthday Parties:** $350 for up to 15 kids, 2 hours exclusive farm access
- **Animals:** Goats, rabbits, chickens, ducks, miniature cow, miniature horse
- **Pony rides:** Hand-led, kids under 75 lbs only
- **Amenities:** Picnic tables, chairs, outdoor fridge/freezer, fire pit, roasting sticks
- **Other services:** Field trips, farm tours, Farm Fun Day, adult classes, summer camps, Nature Night Out, Farm Club

### What They DON'T Have
- No Clydesdales or draft horses
- No wagon rides
- No mobile service (come-to-farm only)
- No branded photo overlays or facial recognition photo delivery
- No corporate event services
- No Indigenous identity or cultural education component

### Threat Assessment: MODERATE

**Why MODERATE (not HIGH):**
- Different model — they're a stationary farm experience, TC is mobile
- No overlap on corporate events, wagon rides, or photo services
- Their pony rides are limited to kids under 75 lbs on a miniature horse — TC offers the full Clydesdale/pony experience for all ages
- No visible content strategy or social media sophistication

**Why not LOW:**
- They are **actively advertising in CDA Facebook groups** — this is direct visibility in TC's market
- $350 for a birthday party is in the same price range TC is targeting for family events
- Families comparing "birthday party with animals" options will see both TC and Happy Found Farm
- They have a real website, FB, and IG presence — more professional than most regional competitors
- They offer summer camps and field trips — potential overlap with TC's school programs

### What TC Should Do About This
1. **Don't panic.** The offerings don't overlap much. TC's Clydesdales, mobile service, and photo tech are in a different league.
2. **Start posting in CDA Facebook groups too.** If Happy Found Farm is advertising there, TC should be visible in the same places. (Remember: Post Falls MARKET for buy/sell, not Post Falls INFO.)
3. **Emphasize what makes TC different in upcoming posts:** "We come to you" / "1,800-lb Clydesdales, not miniature horses" / "Your photos delivered before you leave"
4. **Monitor their Facebook and Instagram** for growth, pricing changes, and service expansion
5. **Consider them a potential collaborator** — they do farm tours and education, TC does mobile events. There might be a referral relationship if the services don't overlap enough to truly compete.
