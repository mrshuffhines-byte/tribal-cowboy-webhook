💰 Financial Terms
  • Deposit: 25% of base fee due upon signing. Secures the event date.
  • Balance: Remaining balance due 14 days before the event date.
  • Optional add-ons (ride sponsorship, additional hours, Clydesdale photo station) invoiced separately and due with balance.
  • Payment methods: Check payable to Tribal Cowboy LLC, ACH transfer, or other method by mutual agreement.
  • Cancellation by Partner (30+ days out): Deposit transfers to a rescheduled date within 90 days.
  • Cancellation by Partner (within 14 days): Full balance is due. No refund of deposit.
  • Cancellation due to weather or emergency before activation begins: Deposit transfers in full to a rescheduled date within 90 days.
  • Cancellation by Tribal Cowboy (force majeure, animal emergency): Full refund of deposit and any balance paid, within 7 days.
🤝 Partner Responsibilities
  • Designate 6–8 parking spaces in the agreed activation footprint (approx. 40' × 60').
  • Provide access to a standard electrical outlet for fans/equipment if available.
  • Designate a named on-site day-of contact and provide name and phone number at least 7 days before event.
  • Approve Tribal Cowboy's event placement map no later than 7 days before event.
  • Provide partner logo file for branded photo overlay no later than 14 days before event.
  • Promote event through owned social media, email, and in-store channels.
  • Allow Tribal Cowboy to reference this partnership in marketing materials and track record documentation (with partner approval on any named references).
🐴 Tribal Cowboy Responsibilities
  • Provide all animals, handlers, panels, equipment, and safety infrastructure.
  • Carry $2M per-occurrence general liability + animal liability endorsement. Name Partner as additionally insured. Deliver Certificate of Insurance 14 days before event.
  • Provide Emergency Action Plan (EAP) to Partner's on-site manager before activation begins.
  • Complete full setup before opening and teardown within 90 minutes of close. No custodial involvement required from Partner.
  • Operate all guest interactions, animal handling, queue management, and photo delivery. Partner staff are not responsible for event operations.
  • Deliver 5 edited event images to Partner within 7 days for marketing use.
  • Deliver written post-event summary within 7 days: attendance, photos delivered, estimated social reach, incident log (if any), and recommendations.
  • Photo system is CCPA-aligned — no guest PII stored or retained, no third-party data sharing.
⚖️ General Terms
  • Independent contractor: Tribal Cowboy LLC operates as an independent contractor. Partner assumes no liability for animal handling, staffing, or event operations.
  • Animal welfare: Tribal Cowboy reserves the right to pause or cancel the activation at any time due to animal distress, weather conditions, or safety concerns. Applicable cancellation terms above apply.
  • Photography & media: Partner may photograph the event for promotional use. Tribal Cowboy may photograph and share event content on owned channels with reasonable prior notice to Partner.
  • Modifications: Changes to event date, scope, or tier must be agreed in writing at least 14 days before the event. Changes within 14 days are subject to availability and may affect pricing.
