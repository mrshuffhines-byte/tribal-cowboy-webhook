## WHAT'S CHANGING AND WHY

The page structure is solid — good headline, clear use cases, clean layout. But the audit flagged three gaps that are costing inquiries:

1. **Pricing says "Contact Us" for every tier.** Photographers and real estate agents compare options quickly. If they can't see a starting price, many move on. Even a "starting at" number dramatically reduces bounce.
2. **Zero testimonials.** Every other service page has social proof. This one has none.
3. **No FAQ section.** Photographers have practical questions (how do horses behave on set? what about indoor shoots? do I need insurance?) and there's nowhere to find answers.
4. **No portfolio/gallery link.** A photographer hiring horse props wants to see what the final shots look like.
5. **All CTAs link to mailto.** Should link to /contact for consistency and lower friction.

**NOT changing:** Hero headline, intro, use case cards, stats bar. All working.

## FIX 3: Add FAQ Section (NEW)

Add after Testimonials, before the final CTA.

**Heading:** Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do the horses behave on a photo set?**
A: Our horses are trained for unfamiliar environments — they've been around cameras, lighting equipment, reflectors, and crew. Clydesdales are naturally calm. That said, we'll talk through your setup ahead of time so there are no surprises for the animal or your team.

**Q: Can you do indoor shoots?**
A: It depends on the space. We need a ground-level entry wide enough for the horse, solid flooring (no carpet or stairs), and enough ceiling height. Most barns, warehouses, and large garages work. Email us your location and we'll tell you if it's feasible.

**Q: Do I need special insurance for having a horse on my set?**
A: We carry our own liability insurance for the animals and handlers. If your venue or client requires additional coverage documentation, let us know ahead of time and we'll coordinate.

**Q: What's your cancellation policy?**
A: We ask for at least 48 hours notice for cancellations or reschedules. Weather-related cancellations are rescheduled at no charge. We know shoots get moved — it happens.

**Q: Can I choose which horse I get?**
A: Yes — if you have a preference for a specific Clydesdale or pony based on size, color, or temperament, tell us when you book. We'll match the animal to your project.

**Q: Do you provide grooming and styling?**
A: Every horse arrives groomed and camera-ready. If you need specific styling (flower crowns, ribbons, seasonal decorations, branded accessories), let us know and we'll prep accordingly. Basic grooming is included; custom styling may be an add-on depending on the request.
