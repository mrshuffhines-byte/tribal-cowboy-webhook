# Tribal Cowboy SEO Audit — April 2026

## Executive Summary

Tribal Cowboy has a **strong foundation** with clear brand positioning, good website structure, and authentic messaging. The site effectively communicates core services (horse photography, pony parties, wagon rides, corporate events) and is capturing search traffic for primary branded and local terms.

**Top 3 Priorities:**
1. **Close the content gap on service-specific long-tail keywords** — competitors rank for "corporate horse event planning," "pony party ideas," "Clydesdale wagon ride near me" but Tribal Cowboy's supporting pages don't target these explicitly
2. **Strengthen local SEO** — North Idaho and Eastern Washington location dominance through optimized local keywords, location pages, and schema markup
3. **Build educational content** — capture "how to plan a horse party," "what to expect at a pony party," "horse event safety tips" — establishes authority and captures early-funnel searches

**Overall Assessment:** Solid SEO fundamentals with clear opportunity to expand reach in service-specific and educational content tiers. No critical technical issues identified.
