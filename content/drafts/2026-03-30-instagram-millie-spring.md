# March 30 — Instagram — The Animals — Millie

Millie spent most of February standing at the fence looking personally offended by the weather.

March arrives and something flips. She's up earlier, moving faster, nose in the new green at the edges of the paddock. Five years old and still surprised every spring that it comes back.

Belle watches from across the field, completely unbothered. She's seen 22 of these.
