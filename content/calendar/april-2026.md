## BEFORE YOU POST — SHOOT THIS ON SATURDAY MARCH 28

**Weather: 62°F, overcast. Best shoot window of the month.**
Overcast = perfect soft light. No harsh shadows. This is better than sunshine for horse photos.

Go outside and capture:
1. **The wagon** — parked, empty, clean. Wide shot showing the whole setup. This is your spring version; swap the fall decor if you can.
2. **The horses being harnessed or in the pasture** — Millie or Abby specifically. Spring coat, spring light.
3. **You with the horses** — even a phone selfie with Millie or Abby works. Spring has them restless, you're getting ready.
4. **Wide shot of the property** — land waking up, fence line, trees starting to bud.

These shots will power content through April and May. Spend 30–45 minutes. Even iPhone is fine for most of these.
