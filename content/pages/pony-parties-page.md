## FIX 2: Add 3 New FAQ Answers

Add these to the existing FAQ section:

**Q: What happens if it rains or the weather is bad?**
A: We keep an eye on the forecast and will reach out ahead of time if weather looks rough. Light rain doesn't bother the ponies — they live outside. If conditions are genuinely unsafe (lightning, heavy storms, extreme heat), we'll reschedule to another date at no charge. We don't cancel for drizzle.

**Q: How much space do you need?**
A: A flat, grassy area about the size of two parking spaces works for most setups. Driveways and patios work too — the ponies aren't picky. We'll confirm setup logistics with you before the event so there are no surprises on the day.

**Q: What if my kid is scared of the horses?**
A: Happens more than you'd think — and it almost always resolves within five minutes. Our handlers are patient and experienced with nervous kids. We let them approach at their own speed. Some start by just watching, then petting, then suddenly they don't want to leave. No pressure, no rushing.
