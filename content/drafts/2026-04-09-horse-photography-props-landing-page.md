## Section 1: How It Works (Process Steps)

**Section Eyebrow:** THE EXPERIENCE

**Section Heading:** You Show Up. We Handle the Rest.

### Step 1: Tell Us Your Vision
Fill out our quick booking form in 2 minutes. Let us know if you want a family session, couples shoot, corporate team photos, or something else. We confirm available dates and send you a price quote within 24 hours.

### Step 2: We Meet You With the Horses
You arrive at the time and place we've arranged (our property, a ranch, a scenic spot in North Idaho—your choice). Our Clydesdales and ponies are groomed, calm, and used to cameras. We spend the first few minutes getting you comfortable with the animals while we scout light and positioning.

### Step 3: We Shoot
You interact naturally with the horses while we capture candid moments. No "say cheese." No standing in a line. We move around, change angles, and catch real reactions—kids laughing, adults who didn't expect to tear up, genuine connection.

### Step 4: Custom Branded Overlay
If you want it, we add your name, event date, or logo to the photos in real-time using our SpotMyPhotos overlay system. It's optional, but most people love it.

### Step 5: Instant Delivery
You scan a QR code right there at the shoot. Your photos appear in your phone instantly. Download them, share them, order prints—whatever you want.

## Section 7: FAQ (Excerpt)

**Q: What if I'm nervous around horses?**
A: You're not alone. We've worked with guests who were terrified. Our horses are calm, trained, and patient. We spend time getting you comfortable before we start shooting. Most nervous people end up enjoying it.

**Q: Can I bring my own photographer?**
A: Absolutely. Some guests want our instant-delivery photos plus professional shots from their own photographer. You get the best of both worlds.

**Q: How long until I get my photos?**
A: Same day. Scan the QR code we provide at the end of your session, and your photos are available to view and download immediately.

**Q: Do I have to buy prints?**
A: No. Downloads are included. You can order prints through our partner or print them yourself. We also offer custom framing suggestions if you want help hanging them.

**Q: What if the weather is bad?**
A: We reschedule. Horses don't like rain either, and we want your session to be comfortable. If weather is unpredictable, we have covered areas on our property, but we prefer sunny, natural light.

**Q: Is this only for families?**
A: No. We do couples sessions, corporate events, milestone celebrations, brand content, and more. Tell us what you need, and we'll figure out if we can deliver it.
