### How It Works

1. You tell us your vision and location
2. We confirm logistics and quote (30 ft outdoor space, grass or dirt preferred)
3. We arrive with horse(s) and any equipment
4. You or your photographer shoot — we manage the animals on-site
5. We pack up and leave. No drama.

The horses are trained and calm on set. We've done this enough times to know what works.
