**Premium Sessions:** $275-$750 range positions above local Coeur d'Alene market ($250-$500/hour) while remaining accessible for families and small groups. Off-property adds $50-$125 premium for travel/setup time.

**Corporate Events:** $500-$950 range reflects professional event coverage, team management, and branded deliverables. Higher tier for 2-hour events and off-property locations.

**Special (Wedding/Education):** Custom pricing allows flexibility for unique needs, non-profit rates, and package deals.

**Add-Ons:** Expedited editing at $150 provides revenue stream for rush requests without adding session time.
