## Meet the Herd

These are the three mini mares who do pony parties. They were born in 2023, which means they are currently about three years old, fully trained, and fully convinced they are the main event — which, honestly, they are.

---

**Reba**

Reba is the smallest of the three. She is aware of this. She uses it strategically. If you are expecting the tiniest horse to be the gentlest horse, Reba would like to recalibrate your expectations. She has the energy of someone who has been underestimated her entire life and is not about to let you forget it. Kids love her because she matches their chaos.

---

**Dolly**

Dolly is beautiful, and she knows it. She walks into a space like she is arriving somewhere important. She will let you admire her. She is perfectly behaved when she decides to be perfectly behaved, which is most of the time. She has decided her own schedule is the relevant one. If Dolly were a person, she would have opinions about table placement at events.

---

**Lainey**

Lainey is the one who will win over the person who "isn't really a horse person." She loves everyone — kids, adults, strangers, people who are nervous, people who don't know what to do with their hands around a horse. She will solve that problem for you. Her signature move: she backs up to whoever is standing nearby, plants her rear end as close as possible, and waits to be scratched. It is not subtle. It is incredibly effective. Multiple grown adults have described Lainey as "the best part of the party," which is a thing someone says about a horse who backs her butt into them unprompted.

## What's Included

**Mini Horse Interactions**
Guests meet Reba, Dolly, and Lainey up close. TC's team manages all animal handling — guests are guided through every interaction. No experience with horses required, and no one is left to figure it out alone.

**Instant Photo Delivery**
Every guest scans a QR code on-site and gets their own photos delivered directly to their phone the same day. No shared album. No waiting. No "can you send me the photos?" follow-up for the next two weeks. The system uses facial recognition to sort and deliver each person's photos to them individually.

**Custom Branded Overlays**
Every photo includes the event name and date printed as a branded overlay. For birthday parties, that means your kid's name is on every photo that leaves the event. For school or community events, the program name travels with the image.

**Full Animal Management**
TC's team is on-site for the entire event. The horses are supervised, repositioned, and managed at all times. This is not a drop-off situation.

**Setup and Breakdown**
TC arrives early, sets everything up, and leaves after full cleanup. The host does not handle any part of the logistics on the day.

## Who It's For

Pony parties work for a range of events — the common thread is that someone is going to see a tiny horse for the first time and completely forget whatever else was happening.

- **Kids' birthdays** — ages 2 through 12 are the sweet spot, though teenagers pretend not to care for approximately 45 seconds before they start asking if they can pet Dolly
- **Mother's Day events** — Reba, Dolly, and Lainey are excellent Mother's Day entertainment and do not require a reservation at a restaurant
- **End-of-school parties** — a real horse is a better reward than a pizza party and every kid knows it
- **Family gatherings and reunions** — works for any age group, including the grandparents who will take more photos than anyone
- **Community events and outdoor festivals** — TC is set up for event-scale crowds and can move guests through efficiently

## What to Expect

**Before the event:** You submit the booking form, TC confirms the date and details, and overlay information is coordinated in advance. For birthday parties, TC needs the child's name and any logo or design elements for the branded overlay.

**Day of the event:** TC arrives before the event start time with the horses already loaded and ready. Setup takes roughly 30–45 minutes. Once the event is running, TC's team manages everything. You are a guest at your own event.

**Photos:** Guests scan the QR code on-site, and photos are delivered directly to their phones within minutes of being taken. TC sends confirmation once delivery is complete.

**Duration:** Base packages run 2 hours. Extended time is available — ask about it when you inquire.
