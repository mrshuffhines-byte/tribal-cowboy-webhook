## Design System (apply to all 3 slides)

**Canvas:** 1080 × 1080 px, RGB, 300 DPI export as PNG for IG.

**Color palette (lock these in Canva Brand Kit):**
- Cream `#F5F0E8` — base background
- Deep Teal `#2D6A6A` — secondary background / blocks
- Gold `#C9A84C` — headline accents, dividers, logo
- Warm Amber `#D4850A` — small highlight pops only
- Soft Gray `#6B7280` — fine print, URL
- White `#FFFFFF` — body text on teal

**Typography (Canva fonts):**
- Headlines: **Playfair Display Bold** (sentence case — never ALL CAPS)
- Body / labels: **Lato Regular** (or Light for secondary lines)
- Max two fonts per slide. Ever.

**Cohesive grid rules (so the 3 slides read as one set):**
- Same 64 px outer padding on every slide
- Same gold hairline divider (2 px) across the top ~120 px down — keeps the eye moving slide to slide
- Small Tribal Cowboy wordmark + URL `tribalcowboy.com` bottom-left on every slide, Lato 14 px, Soft Gray
- Slide indicator dots bottom-right (● ○ ○ / ○ ● ○ / ○ ○ ●) in Gold
- Minimal shadow: if used, 0 blur / 4 px Y / 8% opacity max — flat, modern, not drop-shadow heavy
- No gradients. No stroke text. No glow.

**Imagery direction:**
- Real event photo in the background of slides 1 and 3 (warm-toned, golden-hour if possible)
- Apply a semi-transparent teal overlay at ~55% so headline text reads clean
- Slide 2 is graphic / diagram — no photo background

## Posting Notes

- **Best time to post:** Tuesday or Thursday, 7:30 AM Pacific (pre-commute scroll) or 8:15 PM (evening wind-down). April audience is planning summer events — mid-week + morning wins.
- **First comment** (post immediately after publishing): "Spring dates are open. Link in bio or DM to check availability."
- **Cross-post:** Reformat slide 3 alone as a Facebook post with the same caption — community audience wants the CTA without scrolling.
- **TikTok adaptation:** Turn the 3 slides into a 9-second vertical reel — slide 1 held 2s, slide 2 held 4s (long enough to read cards), slide 3 held 3s with a subtle zoom. Same caption trimmed to the first two paragraphs.
- **Boost candidate:** Yes — $15–25 local boost if it clears 30 saves in the first 24 hours. This is a booking-intent post, not a vanity post.
- **Grid cohesion:** These 3 slides are designed to sit side-by-side on the IG profile grid. If the next post is a photo, place it to the right so the teal of slide 3 anchors the row.
