## How to add in Squarespace (step-by-step)
1. Go to your Squarespace dashboard
2. Click **Pages** in the left sidebar
3. Hover over the page name and click the **gear icon**
4. Click the **SEO** tab
5. Paste the description into the **SEO Description** field
6. Click **Save**
7. Repeat for each page
