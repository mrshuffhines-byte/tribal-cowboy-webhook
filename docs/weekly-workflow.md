# Tribal Cowboy — Weekly Workflow

**Last updated:** 2026-03-21
**Business:** Tribal Cowboy LLC — Stacie Huffhines, Nisenan Maidu
**Design principle:** Low-maintenance, solo-operable, no wasted motion.

---

## Overview

The weekly workflow has three moving parts:
1. **Daily** — 15 minutes or less: check, respond, note
2. **Monday** — 60 minutes: plan the week
3. **Friday** — 30 minutes: review and learn

Everything else in the system supports these three touchpoints. If a week gets away from you, start with Monday. If Monday gets away from you, start with Friday. The system resets weekly.

---

## Daily Routine (15 Minutes or Less)

**When:** Morning, before events or other business tasks. Consistency matters more than the specific hour.

**Tools:** Phone or desktop — wherever TC's social accounts are logged in.

### Step 1: Check Comments and DMs (5–7 minutes)
- Instagram comments on recent posts
- Instagram DMs (including message requests)
- Facebook comments and DMs
- TikTok comments on recent posts
- Story replies (yesterday's stories)

**For each message, ask:** Which intent type is this? (See `docs/engagement-routing-rules.md`)

### Step 2: Respond or Draft Responses (5–7 minutes)
- Tier 1 and Tier 2: respond immediately in-platform — keep it short
- Tier 3: respond if you have the information; if not, send a holding message and note it for follow-up
- Tier 4: note it in your opportunities log (see below), respond with Tier 4 holding message if you need time
- Tier 5: send holding message immediately, flag for deeper attention

**Rule:** Never leave a Tier 5 message unanswered for more than 1 hour.

### Step 3: Note Any Opportunities or Escalations (1–2 minutes)
At the end of the daily check, note anything that needs attention:

**Opportunities log** (running note in phone or doc):
- Partnership inquiry received — [brand name], [date], [status]
- Corporate inquiry received — [company], [date], [status]
- Booking lead received — [date, service type, status]

**Escalations log** (`insights/escalation-log.md`):
- Any Tier 5 situation gets logged with date, nature, and status

## Daily Post (If Posting That Day — 5–10 minutes additional)
If a post is scheduled for today:
- Confirm it is ready in the scheduler (Buffer, Later, or native scheduling)
- Check that hashtags are in the first comment if needed (Instagram)
- After post goes live: check back at 30 minutes and 60 minutes to respond to early comments — this matters for the algorithm

## Monday Planning Session (60 Minutes)

**When:** Sunday evening or Monday morning — whichever is more reliable. The goal is to start the week knowing what is happening.

### Block 1: Review the Content Calendar (10 minutes)
- Open `/TribalCowboy-Social/content/calendar/` — find the current month's calendar
- Confirm what is planned for this week
- Note which posts are drafted and ready vs. still need to be written

**Ask:**
- Does anything need to be updated based on what happened last week?
- Is there a current event, season, or news hook that should change the plan?
- Is the content variety right? (No two posts from the same pillar back to back)

### Block 2: Confirm Drafts Are Ready (10 minutes)
- Open `/TribalCowboy-Social/content/drafts/`
- For each post this week: is the caption written? Are photos/video ready? Are hashtags pulled?
- For any posts not ready: either draft them now or move them on the calendar

**Minimum requirement to call a post "ready":**
- Caption written and reviewed
- Photos selected or video ready
- Hashtags pulled from bank
- CTA (if any) confirmed
- Scheduled in platform or noted as "post live"

### Block 3: Trend Check (10 minutes)
Run the weekly trend check from `docs/trend-intelligence-framework.md`:
- Scroll TikTok FYP (5 minutes) — what format is appearing more than once in TC's topic area?
- Check Instagram Explore or Reels tab (3 minutes) — what are top-performing equine and western posts doing?
- Note anything that passes the four-question filter

**If a trend is worth pursuing:** Add it to the content queue as a draft with a 24-hour deadline.

### Block 4: Review Pending Approvals and Opportunities (10 minutes)
- Any Tier 4 messages from last week still open?
- Any corporate inquiries that need a follow-up or proposal?
- Any creator/brand partnership conversations that need a next step?
- Any escalations still unresolved?

**Action:** Move each one forward by at least one step. Send a follow-up, draft a proposal, or log as resolved.

### Block 5: Creator and Sponsorship Opportunity Check (10 minutes)
- Any active UGC pitches sent? What is their status?
- Any affiliate links needing new content?
- Anything from the creator income roadmap (`docs/creator-income-roadmap.md`) that has a this-week action?
- Any sponsorship conversations to follow up on?

**Action:** Identify one creator-income task to complete this week.

### Block 6: Update the Content Queue (5 minutes)
- Confirm all 3–5 posts for the week are assigned to specific days
- Balance pillar rotation (no duplicates back to back)
- Confirm platform distribution (not all posts on just Instagram)

**Queue format:**
```
MON — Instagram Reel — [topic/pillar] — [status: ready / needs caption / needs photo]
TUE — TikTok Business — [topic/pillar] — [status]
WED — Instagram + Facebook — [topic/pillar] — [status]
THU — TikTok Personal — [topic/pillar] — [status]
FRI — Instagram Reel — [topic/pillar] — [status]
```

### Block 7: Anything Else That Needs Attention (5 minutes)
- Seasonal content — is anything coming up in 2 weeks that needs content now?
- Upcoming events — does anything need a teaser post or Facebook Event created?
- Website — any blog post or service page update pending?

## Friday Review Session (30 Minutes)

**When:** Friday afternoon. The week is behind you; use it before it fades.

### Block 1: What Performed Well This Week (10 minutes)
Check platform insights:
- Instagram: which post had the most reach? Most saves? Most profile visits?
- TikTok: which video had the most views, shares, or new followers?
- Facebook: which post had the most engagement?

**Note in `/TribalCowboy-Social/insights/`:**
- What the post was (pillar, format, topic)
- What the numbers were
- Why you think it performed (hook, topic, format, timing, trend)

### Block 2: What to Reuse or Repurpose (5 minutes)
- Did any post perform well enough to cross-post to another platform?
- Did any post generate a question or comment that deserves its own follow-up post?
- Did any caption line or hook land particularly well? Save it as a template.

**Action:** Add repurpose candidates to next week's content queue.

### Block 3: Leads That Came In This Week (5 minutes)
- Review the opportunities log from daily check-ins
- List all booking inquiries, corporate inquiries, and partnership leads
- What is the status of each?
- What needs to happen next week?

**Booking leads:** Is a deposit confirmed? Is follow-up needed?
**Corporate leads:** Is a proposal sent? Is follow-up needed?
**Partnership leads:** Is a conversation active? What is the next step?

### Block 4: Creator and Partnership Signals (5 minutes)
- Any brand that liked multiple posts, started following, or DM'd this week that could be a future partner?
- Any comment that suggests a media or press interest?
- Any TC content that was shared widely or reposted?

**Action:** Log anything notable. Even if no action yet, the log matters.

### Block 5: One Thing for Next Week (5 minutes)
End the Friday review with one clear decision: what is the most important thing to get right next week?

Examples:
- "Next week I'm sending 3 UGC pitches — get those drafted Monday."
- "The corporate proposal for [company] needs to go out by Tuesday."
- "The spring booking season Reel needs to be shot and posted by Wednesday."

Write it at the top of Monday's planning notes so it's the first thing you see.

## How to Handle an Overwhelming Week

When the week gets away from you and the system feels behind:

**Priority order:**
1. Respond to Tier 5 escalations — these cannot wait
2. Respond to Tier 3 and Tier 4 messages — real leads
3. Post at least 2–3 times (do not go completely dark)
4. Do the Friday review — even 15 minutes is enough
5. Reset on Monday — the system survives imperfect weeks

**Do not:** Try to catch up by posting five times in one day. Inconsistency is better than a posting spike that looks desperate.

## Tools to Support the Workflow

### Scheduling
- Instagram and Facebook: Meta Business Suite (native scheduling — free)
- TikTok: TikTok native scheduler or Later
- Optional: Buffer for cross-platform scheduling

### Drafts
- Saved in `/TribalCowboy-Social/content/drafts/` as `.md` files
- Named by date and platform: `2026-03-24-instagram-spring-booking.md`

### Hashtags
- Bank maintained in `CLAUDE.md` and `docs/channel-strategy.md`
- Pull hashtags by platform at draft time — do not look them up every time

### Insights
- Notes saved in `/TribalCowboy-Social/insights/`
- Simple text files, one per month: `2026-03-insights.md`

### Escalation Log
- `/TribalCowboy-Social/insights/escalation-log.md`
- Running log, never deleted — append new entries at the top
