## Section 1: How It Works (Process Steps)

**Section Eyebrow:** THE EXPERIENCE

**Section Heading:** Here's What a Pony Party Actually Looks Like

### Step 1: Tell Us What You're Planning
Fill out our quick booking form. Tell us: how many kids, what ages, where the party is (your place, a park, a venue), and when. We confirm dates, give you a price quote within 24 hours, and answer any horse-related questions.

### Step 2: We Arrive Early
We show up 30 minutes before guests arrive. Ponies are groomed, calm, and ready. Equipment is set. We scout the space, set up a safe interaction area, and get everything positioned so kids can actually reach and touch the animals without risk.

### Step 3: Kids Meet the Ponies
Kids interact naturally—petting, brushing, feeding treats (we bring them), asking questions, taking photos. We stay with the animals, manage any nervous kids, and keep everyone safe. No boring instruction. Just real moments with animals that are genuinely patient.

### Step 4: Instant Photo Delivery (Optional)
If you want photos, we capture candid shots and add your party theme—balloons, colors, event name, anything you want—using our on-site photo overlay system. You scan a QR code before we leave. Photos are ready to download and share within an hour.

### Step 5: We Clean Up Everything
We break down, haul out, and leave the space cleaner than we found it. You focus on cake and singing. We handle the rest.

## Section 7: FAQ (Excerpt)

**Q: How much does a pony party cost?**
A: Pricing starts at $[XXX] for a 1-hour small party. The final price depends on group size, duration, location, and whether you want the instant photo delivery. Each event is custom, so we quote after understanding what you're planning. No surprises.

**Q: What if the kids are nervous around animals?**
A: We've worked with hundreds of kids. Some come in nervous, and by the end they're asking to come back next week. We take time, move slow, and let kids set the pace. Nervous kids often surprise their parents with how quickly they warm up to the animals.

**Q: Can we do it at our house?**
A: Yes, as long as you have a relatively flat, open area with decent footing (grass or a cleared field). No concrete or steep terrain—that's not safe for the animals or kids. We'll scope it out and confirm when you book.

**Q: What if it rains?**
A: We can reschedule. Horses don't like rain, and we want your party to be comfortable. If you have a covered area on your property or venue, we can sometimes work with that depending on setup. Talk to us about options when you book.

**Q: Do we need to do anything to prepare?**
A: Nope. We handle the animals, equipment, and space setup. You make sure kids have bathroom access and bring the cake. We'll text a simple prep list after you book.

**Q: Can you take photos if we have our own photographer too?**
A: Absolutely. Some families do both—our instant system plus a professional photographer. You get the best of both.

**Q: How do we book?**
A: Email info@tribalcowboy.com or visit www.tribalcowboy.com and fill out the quick form. We confirm dates and send a quote within 24 hours. Easy.
