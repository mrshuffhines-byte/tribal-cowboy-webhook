## Step-by-Step Acuity Setup Instructions

### Step 1: Create Three Service Types

#### Service 1: Horse Photography Session

1. Log into Acuity Scheduler
2. Go to **Settings → Services**
3. Click **Create a New Service**
4. Fill in:
   - **Name:** Horse Photography Session
   - **Duration:** 1 hour
   - **Price:** $350
   - **Description:** *(Copy from "Service 1" section above)*
5. Click **Save**

#### Service 2: General Photography Session

1. Go to **Settings → Services**
2. Click **Create a New Service**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name:** General Photography Session
   - **Duration:** 1 hour
   - **Price:** $300
   - **Description:** *(Copy from "Service 2" section above)*
4. Click **Save**

#### Service 3: Event Photography

1. Go to **Settings → Services**
2. Click **Create a New Service**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name:** Event Photography
   - **Duration:** 2 hours
   - **Price:** $450
   - **Description:** *(Copy from "Service 3" section above)*
4. Click **Save**

---

### Step 2: Add Travel Add-Ons

#### Add-On for Horse Photography & General Photography

1. Go to **Settings → Services → Horse Photography Session → Add-Ons**
2. Click **New Add-On**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name:** Travel to Your Location
   - **Price:** +$50
   - **Description:** "We'll come to you within 20 miles of Athol with full equipment"
4. Click **Save**

5. Repeat for **General Photography Session** with the same add-on

#### Add-On for Event Photography

1. Go to **Settings → Services → Event Photography → Add-Ons**
2. Click **New Add-On**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name:** Travel to Your Location
   - **Price:** +$75
   - **Description:** "We'll bring full event photography coverage to your location within 20 miles of Athol"
4. Click **Save**

---

### Step 3: Set Your Availability

1. Go to **Settings → Availability**
2. For each day of the week, add your time blocks exactly as shown in the table above
3. **IMPORTANT:** Friday needs TWO separate blocks:
   - Block 1: 1:00 PM – 4:00 PM
   - Block 2: 5:00 PM – Sunset
4. Make sure **Tuesday and Thursday are completely closed** (no time blocks)
5. Click **Save**

*Note: These availability settings apply to all three services. If you later want different hours for specific services, you can set service-specific availability in the advanced settings.*

---

### Step 4: Block Race Weekends & Travel Dates

1. Go to **Settings → Calendar & Time Off**
2. For each AUTOSPORTS NORTHWEST AUTOCROSS 2026 race weekend:
   - Create a time-off entry
   - **Type:** Out of Office / Not Available
   - **Date(s):** Race weekend
   - **Mark all day** (or specific hours if you prefer)
3. For any personal travel dates:
   - Create a time-off entry
   - **Type:** Out of Office / Not Available
   - **Date(s):** Your travel dates
4. Click **Save**

---

## Troubleshooting

### Services not showing on public page
- Verify you created all three services (Steps 1)
- Verify you set availability blocks (Step 3)
- Clear your browser cache and refresh

### Pricing not displaying correctly
- Confirm Horse Photography = $350
- Confirm General Photography = $300
- Confirm Event Photography = $450
- Confirm travel add-ons are correct (+$50 and +$75)

### Friday slots won't split into two blocks
- You need to create two separate availability blocks on Friday
- First block: 1:00 PM – 4:00 PM
- Second block: 5:00 PM – [Sunset time]
- If Acuity won't let you create two separate blocks, contact their support

### Race weekends showing as available
- Verify you added them to Time Off in Settings → Calendar & Time Off
- Make sure you marked them as "Not Available"
- Check that the dates are correct
