## Why This Matters

Google Business Profile (formerly Google My Business) is where your business shows up on Google Maps, Google Search, and Google Shopping. When someone searches "pony parties near me" or "horse experiences in Athol, ID," Google uses your Business Profile to show:

- Your location and hours
- Customer reviews and photos
- Services and pricing
- Photos of your horses and past events
- Booking links

Without it, you're invisible for local search. With it, you're the first thing people see.

## Step 1: Create or Claim Your Business Profile

### If you already have one:
1. Go to **https://business.google.com**
2. Click **"Sign in"** with your Google account
3. Look for **Tribal Cowboy LLC** in the list
4. Click it and proceed to Step 2 (Optimization)

### If you don't have one:
1. Go to **https://business.google.com**
2. Click **"Create a business"** or **"+ Create"**
3. Enter:
   - **Business name:** Tribal Cowboy LLC
   - **Address:** Your Athol, ID address
   - **Business category:** Select **"Horse & Pony Rides"** (or closest match)
4. Click **"Next"**
5. Verify your business—Google will ask you to verify by phone or mail
   - **Phone verification is fastest** (2–3 minutes)
   - Address verification (postcard) takes 1–2 weeks
6. Once verified, proceed to Step 2

## Step 4: Posts & Updates

Google Business Profile lets you post updates directly (similar to Facebook). Use this for:

- Upcoming events or availability
- Seasonal updates ("Wagon rides now booking for fall!")
- Photos from recent events
- Special promotions or new services

Posts help with visibility and keep your profile fresh for Google's algorithm.

---

## Step 5: Monitor & Respond to Reviews

**Check weekly:**
1. Go to your Business Profile
2. Click **"Reviews"**
3. Read all new reviews
4. **Respond to every review**—positive or critical

**Sample responses:**

**For 5-star reviews:**
```
Thank you so much, [Name]! We loved meeting [kid's name] and the whole family. Can't wait to see you again! 🐴
```

**For 4-star reviews (address the concern):**
```
Hi [Name], thanks for the great feedback! We appreciate you letting us know about [issue]. We'll definitely [fix/improve] for next time. Hope to see you soon!
```

**For critical reviews:**
Don't get defensive. Be brief, professional, and offer to make it right.
```
Hi [Name], we're sorry to hear you had this experience. That's not how we want families to feel. Please reach out to us at info@tribalcowboy.com so we can make it right.
```

## Step 6: Link Your Website

Once you have your Business Profile set up, add the verification code to your website (Squarespace or wherever it's hosted):

1. In Google Business Profile, look for **"Verification"** or **"Website"** section
2. Copy the HTML verification code
3. Go to your Squarespace Settings > Advanced > Code Injection (or similar)
4. Paste the code
5. Return to Google Business Profile and click "Verify"

This links your website to Google and improves local SEO.

## Common Issues & Fixes

**"My address isn't showing on Google Maps"**
→ Make sure you entered the correct address and verified your business. Give it 24–48 hours.

**"I'm getting reviews in another language"**
→ You can request Google to remove spam/irrelevant reviews.

**"My phone number is wrong"**
→ Go to your Business Profile, click "Edit," and update it immediately. Changes reflect in 1–2 days.

**"I don't have customer reviews yet"**
→ Ask customers directly! Include the Google review link in emails, on your website, or on your business card.
