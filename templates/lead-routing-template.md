## Intent Classification

Check all that apply. Primary intent drives routing decision.

- [ ] **Booking inquiry** — asking about availability, pricing, or how to hire TC
- [ ] **General curiosity** — asking what TC does, how it works, what events look like
- [ ] **Event-specific question** — asking about a specific date, venue, or service type
- [ ] **Creator/media inquiry** — photographer, videographer, blogger, influencer asking about collaboration, press, or content
- [ ] **Partnership or sponsor outreach** — brand, vendor, or org reaching out about working together
- [ ] **Complaint or concern** — something went wrong or a guest has a problem
- [ ] **Spam / irrelevant** — no meaningful connection to TC's services or audience

## Tier Assignment

Assign one tier based on intent and lead quality. When in doubt, go one tier higher.

| Tier | Criteria |
|---|---|
| **Tier 1 — Hot lead** | Specific date, specific service, clear intent to book. Respond within 2 hours during business hours. |
| **Tier 2 — Warm lead** | Asking real questions, not yet ready to book. Engaged, specific. Respond within 24 hours. |
| **Tier 3 — Exploratory** | Curious but vague. No specific date or service. No urgency. Respond within 48 hours with education-first response. |
| **Tier 4 — Creator/Partner** | Inbound media, creator, or partnership inquiry. Route to partnership evaluation template before responding. |
| **Tier 5 — Hold or ignore** | Spam, bots, irrelevant pitches, or anything requiring legal review before response. |

## Routing Action

Select one:

- [ ] **Respond now** — Tier 1 or time-sensitive. Use standard response bank or draft custom reply.
- [ ] **Move to inquiry path** — Tier 2 or 3. Send intake or ask qualifying questions before committing to a quote.
- [ ] **Escalate** — Tier 4. Pull up partnership outreach template or creator opportunity evaluation template.
- [ ] **Hold for manual review** — Complaint, legal concern, or anything sensitive. Log in escalation log before acting.
- [ ] **No response needed** — Spam or irrelevant. Log and close.
