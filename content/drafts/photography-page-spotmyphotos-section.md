## Section Headline

**Your photos are on your phone before you leave.**

## Section Body

TC uses facial recognition technology to deliver every guest's photos directly to their phone the same day — no shared album, no email follow-up, no waiting until next week.

Here is how it works:

A QR code is displayed at the event. Guests scan it once. The system identifies their face across every photo taken that day and delivers their photos directly to them — just theirs, not everyone else's. Delivery typically happens within minutes of the photos being taken.

There is no photographer chasing people down for contact information. There is no shared drive where guests have to scroll through 400 photos to find their own. There is no "I'll send them when I get home" that turns into three weeks later.

Every photo also includes a custom branded overlay — the event name, date, and any logo or design elements provided in advance. For a child's birthday party, that means the child's name is on every photo that goes home. For a corporate event, the company name travels with every image a guest shares.

---

## Why This Matters for Your Event

Most events end with photos that half the guests never see. Either the photographer takes weeks to deliver them, the shared album gets lost, or people just forget to look. TC's photo delivery is built so that doesn't happen — the photos reach every guest the same day, while the event is still something they want to share.

For corporate events and school programs, this is a measurable outcome: every guest leaves with branded content on their phone that they took themselves, delivered without any action required on the event organizer's part.

---

## What You Provide

Before the event, TC coordinates the branded overlay with you — event name, date, and any logo or design file. That's it. TC handles the rest on the day.
