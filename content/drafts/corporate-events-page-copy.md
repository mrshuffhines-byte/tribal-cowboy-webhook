## How Instant Photo Delivery Works

TC uses facial recognition to identify every guest's photos and deliver them directly to their phones the same day — and that is what separates TC's corporate events from every other equine or entertainment option in the region.

Here is what it means in practice: every guest at your event scans a single QR code, and the system identifies and delivers their photos directly. No email list collection. No shared drive. No having to remember which photos were theirs. Delivery happens the same day — often within minutes of the photos being taken.

Every photo has your company logo and event date printed as a branded overlay. That image goes home in every guest's phone. It gets texted to a spouse, posted to Instagram, used as a profile photo, shown to a neighbor. Your company's name travels with it.

For an event budget approval, this is a different kind of return than standard entertainment. TC is not just providing an activity — it is generating branded photo content that your attendees distribute for you.
