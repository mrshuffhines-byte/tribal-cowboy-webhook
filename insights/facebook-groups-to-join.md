## How to Use These Groups

**When you join:**
1. Read the group rules first — every group has different posting guidelines
2. Introduce yourself for real. Not a sales pitch. Just who you are and what you do
3. Engage with other people's posts before promoting your own stuff
4. Post once or twice a month with real content — event photos, behind-the-scenes, the animals
5. Use the "promote your business" threads when they come up (like Rathdrum Community News)
6. Don't hard sell. Let the photos do the work

**Best photos to use in group posts:**
- The South Boundary Holiday Festival branded overlay photos (shows the product)
- Birthday pony party photos (shows the service)
- The "Real Animals. Instant Photos." graphic (for event planner groups)
- Golden hour animal photos (for horse/equine groups)

**Best comment template for "promote your business" threads:**

> Tribal Cowboy here, based in Athol. We do portrait sessions, mini sessions with real horses and ponies, and event photography with custom branded overlays for your business or event. Parties, festivals, farmers markets, corporate events — we show up with the animals and the camera. Guests scan a QR code and get their photos delivered straight to their phone. Great for realtors, small businesses, anyone who wants to stand out. www.tribalcowboy.com
