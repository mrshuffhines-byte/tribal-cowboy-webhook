## General Notes on Outreach

- **Send in batches of 5.** Don't blast 20 at once. Follow up personally.
- **Best time to send:** Tuesday–Thursday, 8–10am or 1–3pm
- **Attach the one-pager PDF** once we build it (see corporate-sponsor-pitch-page.md for content)
- **Track responses** in a simple spreadsheet: Company | Contact | Date sent | Response | Next step
- **Decision timeline:** Chambers and city events book 3–6 months out. Pitch now for summer. Pitch summer for fall.
