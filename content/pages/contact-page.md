## WHAT'S CHANGING AND WHY

The Contact page works, but the form is too generic. Right now it's Name, Email, Message — that's it. Every inquiry comes in with the same blank "Message" field, which means Stacie has to email back asking the same follow-up questions every time: What service? What date? How many guests? Where?

Adding a few structured fields means:
- Stacie gets the info she needs to quote on the first reply
- The customer feels like this business has its act together
- Fewer back-and-forth emails = faster bookings

**Not changing:** Hero headline ("Let's talk horses."), hero image, contact info sidebar, response time language. All of that is working.
