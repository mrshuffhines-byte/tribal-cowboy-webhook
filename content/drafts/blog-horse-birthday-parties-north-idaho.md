## ARTICLE

Your kid wants horses at their birthday party. You're not sure what that looks like, how much it costs, or whether you're going to spend the whole time worrying someone's going to get hurt. Fair. Here's what actually happens when you book a pony party with Tribal Cowboy in North Idaho.

### Not All Pony Parties Are the Same

There are a few people who do pony rides for birthday parties in North Idaho. We know because we're one of them. Most of them haven't invested in the gear or the training that makes this safe and worth paying for.

We use custom child-sized saddles that actually fit kids. Not a cheap western saddle slapped on a pony that doesn't fit the horse or the rider. Our saddles come with interchangeable pommels that we swap based on the child's age and riding ability. A two-year-old sitting on a pony for the first time needs a completely different setup than an eight-year-old who's been around horses. We adjust for every kid.

We have three saddle colors. Pink, purple, and black. So if your party has a unicorn theme (and let's be honest, it probably does), the saddle matches. We also bring unicorn horns, flower crowns, ribbons, mane braids, and themed saddle pads. The ponies show up looking like they belong at the party, not like they just came off a trail ride.

Every child wears a helmet. We provide them in multiple sizes. This isn't optional.

### What We Actually Recommend

Every party is different. Some kids want to be on a horse. Some just want to pet one. Most parties have a mix of ages and sizes. Here's how we help you get the most out of your booking.

**Horse Photography Experience.** This is what I recommend first. We bring a pony, dress them up, set up a small staged area, and your kids get professional photos with a real horse. Individual shots, candid moments, custom overlay with the party name and date. Photos go straight to parents' phones the same day through a QR code. No waiting two weeks for a photographer to send files. This works for small parties or big ones, and you walk away with something you keep forever. That's the biggest bang for your buck.

**Mini Pony Wagon Rides.** A small wagon pulled by our ponies that fits 4 kids or 2 adults and 2 children at once. Moves more kids through faster than individual rides, and perfect for little ones who want a parent right next to them. If you've got a bigger group or a wide age range, this is the smarter pick. No weight limit worries. Everyone gets a turn.

Both options can be combined with enough lead time.

**What about pony rides?** Riding is available as an add-on for $50 per party. We bring one designated riding pony with our custom-fitted saddles, interchangeable pommels, and helmets. But here's the reality most people don't think about: ponies max out at about 75 lbs. Most birthday parties have kids ranging from 2 to 10, and the size difference is huge. The bigger kids can't ride. For a mixed-age group, wagon rides or photos usually end up being the better fit, and every kid gets a turn.

You need flat outdoor space. Backyard, a park, a church parking lot. We've done all three.

### Ages and Group Size

Ages 2-10. Max 15 kids. This keeps it safe and makes sure every child gets real time with the horses, not a rushed 30-second ride while 20 other kids wait in line.

### What Does It Cost?

Pony parties start at $350 for two hours. That includes the ponies, handlers, themed accessories, and full setup and teardown. No riding in the base package. You're booking a crew that shows up, runs the whole thing, and cleans up after.

Add pony rides for $50 (one riding pony per party, 75 lb max). Pricing goes up depending on party length, guest count, and add-ons. Most birthday parties run 1-2 hours.

### What About Safety?

This is the thing we take most seriously and the thing most parents don't know to ask about.

Our ponies are trained specifically for this work. Monster Unicorn has had balloons, screaming kids, and party poppers around him and has never spooked. That's not luck. That's years of conditioning and the right temperament.

If you add riding, we don't cut corners. A properly fitted saddle keeps a child centered and secure. An ill-fitting saddle on an ill-fitting horse is how kids fall. We match the saddle, the pommel, and the horse to the child. Every time. Helmets provided in every size. No exceptions.

We also never rush. If a child is nervous, they approach at their own pace. No one gets forced into anything. Usually the scared kid is petting the pony within 10 minutes and doesn't want to leave.

Summer is peak season. Book 4-8 weeks out for a specific date. Spring and fall have more flexibility.
