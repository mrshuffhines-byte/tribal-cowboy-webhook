Design Notes for Print Production
Print Specs
• Final file size: 48″ W × 138″ H (11′6″ tall)
• Resolution: 150 dpi minimum (300 dpi preferred)
• File format: PDF (print-ready) or PNG
• Bleed: Add 0.5″ bleed on all sides per 48hourprint specs
• Color mode: CMYK
• Safe zone: Keep all text 1″ from all edges

Recommended Upgrade
Replace the SVG horse silhouettes with cutout photos of Millie, Abby, and your ponies at their correct heights. A real photo of Millie at 17H will stop kids and adults cold — no illustration can match it. Use a white background removal tool (remove.bg) to create transparent PNGs.
