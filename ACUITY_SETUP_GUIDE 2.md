## Step-by-Step Acuity Setup

### Step 1: Create the Service Type

1. Log into Acuity Scheduler
2. Go to **Settings → Services**
3. Click **Create a New Service**
4. Fill in:
   - **Name:** Horse Photography Props Session
   - **Duration:** 1 hour
   - **Price:** $350
5. Click **Save**

### Step 2: Add the Horses Add-On

This allows clients to upgrade from $350 to $450 if they want horses included.

1. Go to **Settings → Services → [Your service] → Add-Ons**
2. Click **New Add-On**
3. Fill in:
   - **Name:** Add Horses
   - **Price:** +$100 (this increases the session price to $450)
4. Click **Save**

### Step 3: Set Your Availability

1. Go to **Settings → Availability**
2. For each day (Monday through Sunday), add your time blocks exactly as shown in the table above
3. **Important:** Friday needs TWO separate blocks:
   - Block 1: 1:00 PM – 4:00 PM
   - Block 2: 5:00 PM – Sunset
4. Click **Save**

### Step 4: Block Race Weekends

Block out all AUTOSPORTS NORTHWEST AUTOCROSS 2026 race weekends:

1. Go to **Settings → Calendar & Time Off**
2. For each race weekend, create a time-off entry:
   - **Type:** Out of Office / Not Available
   - **Date:** [Race weekend date]
   - **Mark all day** or specific hours (your choice)
3. Click **Save**

*Tip: Check your personal calendar for all 2026 race dates and add them to Acuity as you compete.*

### Step 5: Test Your Setup

1. Go to your **public booking page** (the link clients will use)
2. Verify:
   - ✓ Slots show correctly for each day
   - ✓ Friday has two separate time blocks (1–4 PM and 5 PM–Sunset)
   - ✓ Race weekends are blocked and unavailable
   - ✓ Price shows as $350 (or $450 with "Add Horses" selected)

## Important Notes

### Golden Hour Pricing
Friday 5 PM–Sunset slots are the same price as regular slots ($350 or $450 with horses add-on). No premium charge.

### Booking Buffer
Consider adding a 15–30 minute buffer between bookings if you need travel time to different photo locations.

### Custom Branding in Acuity
Customize your confirmation email and booking page to highlight your unique offering:

> "You'll receive instant digital delivery with custom branded overlays featuring your event name, logo, and date—available via QR code right after your session."

### Sunset Times (Seasonal)
Since Friday slots end at "Sunset," you may want to adjust the end time in Acuity seasonally:
- **Summer:** ~9:00 PM
- **Spring/Fall:** ~7:00 PM
- **Winter:** ~5:30 PM

Check IdahoSunsets.com or similar for your specific dates.

### Confirmation & Booking Details
In Acuity's confirmation email, include:
- **What to expect:** Candid-style photography with horses
- **Photo delivery:** Custom branded digital photos via QR code (instant)
- **Cancellation policy:** [Your policy]
- **Directions:** Link to tribalcowboy.com or Google Maps location

## Troubleshooting

### Clients can't see available times
- Verify you added availability blocks (step 3)
- Check that race weekends are blocked (step 4)
- Make sure the time blocks are in your local timezone

### Price not showing correctly
- Confirm the service price is set to $350 (step 1)
- Confirm the add-on price is +$100 (step 2)

### Friday slots won't split into two blocks
- You may need to create them as separate "time off" periods or use Acuity's advanced scheduling
- Contact Acuity support if needed
