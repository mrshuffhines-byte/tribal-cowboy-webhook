# DM Reply Bank — Tribal Cowboy LLC

**How to use this:** These are starting points, not scripts. Read the inquiry first. Pick the closest template. Adjust for anything specific they mentioned — their kid's name, their city, their event date. Send something that sounds like a person wrote it, because one did.

**Tone rules:** Warm but not gushing. Confident but not salesy. Give them what they need to take the next step — don't over-explain, don't pressure, don't flood them with options before they're ready.

**Pricing rule:** Never quote a price in a DM before understanding the event. Always direct to www.tribalcowboy.com or invite them to share details first.
