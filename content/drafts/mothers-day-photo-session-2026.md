## The Concept: "Real Mothers"

**The hook:**
Tribal Cowboy has two mother-daughter Clydesdale pairs — Jill & Abby (13 and 7), and Belle & Millie (22 and 5). No other equine business in North Idaho has this. That's the session.

Moms bring their kids. They meet real horse mothers and daughters. The photos tell the story without anyone having to explain it.

**Why it works:**
- The parallel is immediately emotional without being sentimental or cliché
- It's specific — "Jill is Abby's mom" is more compelling than "beautiful horses"
- It positions the Clydesdales as characters, not props
- SpotMyPhotos delivers every photo before the family leaves — that's the closer
- Nobody else is doing this in the region

**Service structure:**
- Mini sessions: 30 minutes per family, 4–6 slots across a 2–3 hour window
- Clydesdales: rotate between Jill & Abby and Belle & Millie depending on how each pair is that day
- Photo delivery: SpotMyPhotos facial recognition — photos on phones before they drive away
- Custom overlay: "Mother's Day 2026 | Tribal Cowboy" on every image
- Price: $150–$175 per family slot (30 min, full photo delivery included)
- Location: TC property in Athol
