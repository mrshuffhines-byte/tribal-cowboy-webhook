## What to Pair With These Posts

Photos that perform well for this audience:
- Millie or Abby standing next to a person in business-casual clothes (shows scale + approachability)
- Guest reaction shots — first-time horse encounter
- Phone showing the branded photo delivery screen
- Wide shot of the setup with people milling around (shows it works as an event, not just a photo op)

If you don't have photos with corporate-looking guests yet, the pony party or community event photos still work — lead with the branded delivery angle, not the Western aesthetic.
