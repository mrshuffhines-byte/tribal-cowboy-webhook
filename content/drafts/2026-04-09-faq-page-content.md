## FAQ Content

### **Pony Parties**

**Q: How much does a pony party cost?**

A: Pony party pricing starts at $[XXX] for a 1-hour event. The price depends on how many kids, how long you want the experience, and where you're located (we serve North Idaho and Eastern Washington). Each event is customized, so contact us at info@tribalcowboy.com or visit www.tribalcowboy.com to get a quote.

---

**Q: What's included in a pony party?**

A: Each pony party includes hands-on time with our gentle ponies—kids can pet, brush, and interact with the animals. We handle all setup and cleanup. You bring the cake and party games; we bring the horses and the memories. If you want photos, we can add instant photo delivery with our photo overlay system (branded with your party theme).

---

**Q: How old do kids need to be?**

A: Kids as young as 2 can enjoy a pony party (with parent help for the littlest ones). We've done hundreds of parties for kids 2–12. Teenagers and adults enjoy them too if you're planning a family event or corporate outing.

---

**Q: Do you bring horses to our location?**

A: Yes. We bring our ponies and all equipment to your venue—home, park, school, or event space. We need a relatively flat, open area with good footing (grass, arena, or cleared field). We don't do concrete or steep terrain for safety reasons.

---

**Q: What if it rains?**

A: We can reschedule or, depending on the venue, set up under a covered area. Just let us know your preferences when you book.

---

**Q: How do I book?**

A: Reach out to us at info@tribalcowboy.com or check availability at www.tribalcowboy.com. We'll talk through dates, location, and what you're looking for, then confirm and send you a contract.

---

### **Horse Photography Props**

**Q: How does the horse photography session work?**

A: You book a session time, and we meet you with one or more of our horses as your photo props. We take candid shots of you or your family with the horses in a natural setting. It's all you and the animal—no posed, stiff stuff. We deliver instant photos via our mobile photo system (you get them right there at the session), and you can order prints or digital downloads.

---

**Q: How long is a photo session?**

A: Sessions are typically 30–60 minutes, depending on what you want. More time = more photos and more outfit or location changes.

---

**Q: Can you add a custom photo overlay?**

A: Yes. We can add your name, event date, or a custom branded overlay to the photos. Great for family photos, corporate events, or weddings. Let us know when you book.

---

**Q: What should I wear?**

A: Wear what makes you comfortable around horses. Avoid overly bright or flashy patterns (they can be distracting in photos). Solid colors, jeans, flannel, and casual styles photograph well. Bring a jacket or change of clothes if you want variety in the shots.

---

**Q: How do I get my photos?**

A: We use a mobile photo delivery system. Scan a QR code right after your session, and your photos are available to view and download instantly. You can order prints or just download digital files—your choice.

---

**Q: Can I bring my own photographer?**

A: Yes, you can bring your own photographer, or use ours. Some folks do both—they get candid shots from our system plus professional shots from their own photographer. Either way works.

---

### **Clydesdale Wagon Rides**

**Q: What's the Clydesdale wagon ride experience?**

A: It's a chance to experience what heritage transportation was really like. You ride in an authentic wagon pulled by our Clydesdale horses across beautiful North Idaho property. It's peaceful, scenic, and a total change of pace from everyday life.

---

**Q: How long is a wagon ride?**

A: Rides are typically 30–45 minutes depending on the route and your preferences. Longer rides available by request.

---

**Q: Is it safe for kids?**

A: Yes, wagon rides are family-friendly. Kids love it. Clydesdales are gentle giants, and we've done hundreds of rides safely. There are no age restrictions, but very young kids should sit with a parent.

---

**Q: What time of year do you do wagon rides?**

A: Spring through early fall is our main season. Winter rides are possible depending on conditions. Reach out to check availability for the season you're interested in.

---

**Q: Can we do a private wagon ride?**

A: Yes. We offer private rides for families, couples, or groups. Pricing depends on group size and route length. Contact us for details.

---

### **Corporate & Team-Building Events**

**Q: What kind of corporate events can you do?**

A: We've done team-building events, off-site retreats, client appreciation events, holiday parties, and more. Whether you want everyone on a wagon ride together, a group pony party, or horse photography props for team photos, we can customize it.

---

**Q: How many people can you accommodate?**

A: Groups of 10–100+. We have multiple horses and can scale the experience based on your group size. Larger events may require multiple time slots or sessions.

---

**Q: What's the best team-building activity with horses?**

A: It depends on your goals. If it's about bonding and having fun, pony parties or group wagon rides work great. If it's about a unique photo experience for marketing or team morale, horse photography is perfect. Talk to us about what you're trying to achieve, and we'll design something that fits.

---

**Q: Do you have liability insurance?**

A: Yes. We're fully insured and require event waivers for all participants.

---

### **General Questions**

**Q: What makes Tribal Cowboy different?**

A: We're Indigenous-owned (Nisenan Maidu) and run by someone who's been around horses her whole life. Our Clyesdales and ponies are part of our family, not rentals—you can tell by how calm and social they are. Plus, we offer instant photo delivery on-site, which most other horse experience businesses don't have.

---

**Q: Are the horses well-cared for?**

A: Absolutely. These are our animals. They're groomed, trained, and loved. We don't overwork them, we feed them well, and we retire them when their time comes. If you're worried about animal welfare, come meet them—you'll see.

---

**Q: Do you serve Eastern Washington?**

A: Yes. We regularly travel throughout Eastern Washington for events. Distance affects pricing, but we're happy to work with you if you're in our service area (roughly 1–2 hours from Athol, ID).
