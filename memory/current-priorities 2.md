## What NOT to Do This Month

**1. Do not run a discount or early-bird pricing promotion.**
TC's positioning is premium. A discount this early in the season communicates that pricing was too high to begin with. It attracts price-sensitive buyers and repels the premium clients TC wants for summer. The right clients will pay the right price. Let the content do the selling.

**2. Do not post daily just to hit a number.**
Quality beats frequency. Three strong, specific, on-brand posts per week are worth more than seven generic ones. If there's nothing real to say, don't post. The algorithm punishes weak content more than it punishes gaps.

**3. Do not accept gifting-only brand deals this month.**
March and April are early enough in the year to hold out for paid partnerships. Accepting boots-for-tags now sets a precedent and lowers TC's perceived value for the larger deals that should be coming in Q2. If the brand isn't offering payment, the answer is no or a counterproposal, not yes.
