## Key Research Insights Driving This Schedule

1. **Completion rate > likes.** Keep videos tight. 70% watch-through is the viral threshold. Every second earns its spot.
2. **"Slow content" is trending.** Process videos, day-in-the-life, unhurried explanations outperform rapid-fire edits.
3. **Original audio is strong.** Talk-to-camera performs as well as trending sounds right now.
4. **Size-comparison content stops the scroll.** Clydesdales next to anything = built-in hook.
5. **North Idaho TikTok is empty.** Local geographic tags have almost zero competition from event vendors. First-mover advantage.
6. **Indigenous + horses + business = untouched niche.** Nobody is making this exact content. The Indigenous creator community is supportive and cross-promotes.
7. **No party footage constraint is not a problem.** The top-performing formats in this space (direct-to-camera, horse personality, grooming, barn life, educational) don't require event footage at all.
