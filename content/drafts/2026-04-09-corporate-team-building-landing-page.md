## Section 1: How It Works (Corporate Process)

**Section Eyebrow:** THE EXPERIENCE

**Section Heading:** Here's What a Corporate Team-Building Day Actually Looks Like

### Step 1: We Understand Your Team & Goals
Email or call us with group size, available dates, and what you're hoping for. (Some companies want pure fun. Others want an icebreaker before a planning session. We work with it all.) We confirm availability and send a custom quote within 24 hours.

### Step 2: We Scout & Customize the Space
Whether you're meeting at a ranch property, corporate retreat center, park, or your own venue, we visit or video-tour the space. We'll position the horses and handlers to maximize interaction, ensure safety, and fit your group size and schedule.

### Step 3: Your Team Meets the Horses
Groups of 10–30 work best for true engagement. Smaller groups get more hands-on time; larger groups stay broken into natural sub-groups. People pet, brush, feed treats, ask questions, take photos. We manage all animal care and group flow. No previous horse experience needed.

### Step 4: Optional Photo Capture & Branding
Some companies want candid event photos with their logo or company name branded into the shots via QR code delivery. We capture and deliver the same day. Others skip this and just enjoy the moment. Your call.

### Step 5: We Break Down & You Refocus
We handle all equipment cleanup and animal transport. Your team can debrief over lunch, move into a planning session, or just ride the high back to the office knowing they did something together that wasn't a typical team outing.
