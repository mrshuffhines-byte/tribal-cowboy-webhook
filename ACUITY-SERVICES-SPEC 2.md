## PRICING RATIONALE (Coeur d'Alene Market Analysis)

**Market Context:**
- Local photography average: $250-$500/hour
- Premium boutique photography: $500-$1,200/hour  
- Tribal Cowboy differentiation: Unique equine element, Indigenous-owned, instant delivery with facial recognition, custom branded overlays, professional editing

**Our Positioning:**
- Premium tier (higher end of local market)
- $425/hour base for 1-hour sessions
- $750+ for corporate 2-hour minimum
- Full-day rates ($725-$1,200) competitive with regional wedding/event photographers

**Justification for Pricing:**
1. **Unique offering** - No competitors in North Idaho offering equine photography + instant delivery
2. **Technology cost** - SpotMyPhotos facial recognition + custom overlay branding
3. **High-touch service** - Professional editing, custom graphics, personal service
4. **Premium positioning** - Attracts corporate events, weddings, lifestyle shoots (higher margin)
5. **Indigenous-owned** - Cultural authenticity and story value

## BOOKING WORKFLOW AFTER SETUP

1. **Customer visits website** → Sees service description + pricing
2. **Clicks "Book Now"** → Goes to Acuity booking page
3. **Selects date/time** → Calendar shows availability
4. **Fills intake form** → Provides event details, logo, preferences
5. **Completes payment** → 50% deposit or full payment (configure)
6. **Gets confirmation email** → Includes SpotMyPhotos QR code info
7. **Event day** → Tribal Cowboy photographs with branded overlays
8. **Photos delivered** → Via SpotMyPhotos + private gallery link within 24-48 hours
