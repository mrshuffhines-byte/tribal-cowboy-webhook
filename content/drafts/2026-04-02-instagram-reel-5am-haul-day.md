## Visual Plan

Film the real sequence, in order:
1. Alarm clock or phone showing early time (2 seconds)
2. Loading equipment into the trailer — branded overlays, photo gear, grooming supplies (5 seconds)
3. Horses loading up — show the size of Millie or Abby stepping into the trailer (5 seconds)
4. Pull out of the driveway in the dark or early light (3 seconds)
5. Arrive at the event venue — empty parking lot, nobody there yet (3 seconds)
6. Final shot: first guest arrives, kid sees the horses (3 seconds)

Sound: real audio — trailer latches, hoofbeats on the ramp, truck engine. No music, or very minimal.

Text overlay on the last frame: "They just see the horses."
