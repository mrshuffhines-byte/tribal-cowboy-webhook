## About / Long Description
*(Paste into: Edit Page Info → About)*

Tribal Cowboy is an Indigenous-owned equine experience company based in Athol, Idaho — Nisenan Maidu led, veteran family rooted in North Idaho.

We bring horses to corporate events, chamber activations, grand openings, luxury parties, school programs, and community events across North Idaho and Eastern Washington. Clydesdales, ponies, wagon rides, and branded photo experiences — fully managed, insured, and handled start to finish.

We're building a therapy ranch in memory of Stacie's brother Billy, who had Down syndrome and passed away in 2025. The ranch will provide accessible horse experiences and respite for individuals with special needs and their caregivers. Goal: fully running by 2027. Every appearance moves that forward.

For corporate sponsorships, chamber partnerships, and brand activations: info@tribalcowboy.com

Book an event: tribalcowboy.as.me
