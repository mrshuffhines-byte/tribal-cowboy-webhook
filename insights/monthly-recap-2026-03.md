**National pony party landscape (from #ponyparty research):**
- Competitors found: @edenequine1, @rockinghorse_stables, @pony.palooza.llc, @jennyspartynponyrental, @pictureperfectponyss, @mylittleponyexperience

- Nobody in the #ponyparty space mentions branded photo overlays, facial recognition delivery, or Indigenous ownership.
- Carousels dominate the higher-engagement posts in this hashtag. Story-forward captions that name the birthday child outperform generic "book us" posts.

**Bottom line:** TC has no direct competitor in this region and multiple differentiators that are absent from the national pony party space. The gap is awareness, not competition. April's content push is the right next move.
